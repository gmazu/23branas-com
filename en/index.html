What currently defines the framework

The current priority is not to “explain everything” at once, but to close pieces with mechanism: collision geometry, observational scales, medium perturbations, experimental analogies, and documents that can be read without depending on a previous conversation.

The collision is not treated as an explosion without mechanism, but as a physical interface sweeping a geometry.
eCEL is used as a liberated regime of the medium, with propagation, coupling, and wave-like response.
Apparent inflation is read geometrically, not as matter moving faster than light.
The same architecture tries to connect cosmological structure, gravitational waves, and more microscopic branches of the model.

Reading Summary
1

Antimatter drop

The upper drop is read as the antimatter regime entering or coupling with the lower structure.

2

Matter universe

The ocean-like field carrying the visible wave is read as the matter universe.

3

Observed / unobserved width

The width is not read as a single uniform universe. One sector corresponds to the observable universe, while the remaining extension belongs to the unobserved universe.

4

First front

A first propagation front appears close to the center and may define the faster regime.

5

Measured front

A second front is the one effectively measured, which helps explain why two apparent velocities are inferred.

6

Observer region

The working hypothesis is that we are located somewhere between those two fronts. If the gap is modeled, the relative observer position might be estimated.

Current state

The priority is still to close readable documents, not to inflate the system with new branches. The question is no longer “is there an idea?”, but which part is already closed enough to be read without extra context.

Collision of 2 3-Branes is already in a polishing and external-reading phase.
MazzGAP works as a separate technical branch for stability and binding energy.
The website should act as an entry point, not as a substitute for the paper.
