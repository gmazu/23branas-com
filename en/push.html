How To Read

If the row is P and the column is e-, the cell reads P R1 e-. In this matrix, 1 means active structural interaction and 0 means no direct interaction is counted in this layer.

Document Reading
  • Nucleus: P with P+.
  • Atom: P+ with e-.
  • Released membranes are not calculated in R1.
  • The neutrino e participates as a neutral state in the interaction layer.
Active Combinations
  • P with P, P+, e-, e.
  • P+ with P, P+, e-, e.
  • e- with P, P+, e-, e.
  • e with P, P+, e-, e.
