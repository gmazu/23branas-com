Reading Summary
1

Antimatter drop

The upper drop is read as the antimatter regime entering or coupling with the lower structure.

2

Matter universe

The ocean-like field carrying the visible wave is read as the matter universe.

3

Observed / unobserved width

The width is not read as a single uniform universe. One sector corresponds to the observable universe, while the remaining extension belongs to the unobserved universe.

4

First front

A first propagation front appears close to the center and may define the faster regime.

5

Measured front

A second front is the one effectively measured, which helps explain why two apparent velocities are inferred.

6

Observer region

The working hypothesis is that we are located somewhere between those two fronts. If the gap is modeled, the relative observer position might be estimated.
